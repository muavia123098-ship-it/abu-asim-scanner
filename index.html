🎙️ Speaking Guide

How to use Voice Commands

Simply tap the large glowing Microphone button at the bottom and speak directly! For example:

English Example
"200 plus 350 plus 1200 minus 300 equal"
Calculates: 200 + 350 + 1200 - 300 = 1450
Urdu/Hindi Example
"nau sau jamah do sau nifi pachas barabar"
Calculates: 900 + 200 - 50 = 1050

Supported Spoken Keywords

Math Operation English Keywords Urdu/Hindi Keywords
